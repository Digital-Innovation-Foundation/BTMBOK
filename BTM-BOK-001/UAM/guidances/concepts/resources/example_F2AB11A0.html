Concept: Example

Main Description

An Example represents a typical, partially completed, sample instance of one or more Content Elements. Examples are most commonly provided for Work Products. A Work Product Example is a good supplement to its prescriptive and descriptive process Guidance.

Examples should be associated with specific Work Products to give their producer a view of how it should look like when it is done.
