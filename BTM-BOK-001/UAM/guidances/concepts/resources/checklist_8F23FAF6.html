Concept: Checklist
A Checklist identifies a series of items that need to be completed or verified. Checklists are often used in reviews such as walkthroughs or inspections.
Main Description

In UMA, a Content Element has at most one Checklist. Checklists, are useful in a number of contexts: they help you decide what to do, they help doing it, they help assess the quality of the work, and they help understand how a particular Work Product relates to the rest of the process.

Work products typically have an associated Checklist which present information on how to develop, evaluate and use them .
